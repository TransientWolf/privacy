Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Data Collection

My Digital Garage does not collect, track, or sell personal data.

All vehicle details, documents, images, and profile information you enter are stored locally on your device and, if enabled, in your private iCloud storage.

iCloud Sync

If iCloud sync is enabled, your data is stored in your personal Apple iCloud container using CloudKit.

Only you can access this data through your Apple ID. The developer cannot access your content.

Documents & Images

Any photos or scanned documents you add remain on your device and/or your private iCloud storage.

They are not uploaded to third-party servers and are not analysed or shared.

Notifications

The app may generate local notifications for reminders such as MOT expiry, tax renewal, insurance renewal, service due dates, and driving licence expiry.

These notifications are created entirely on-device.

Analytics & Tracking

My Digital Garage uses no analytics SDKs, performs no tracking, and contains no advertising.

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by removing vehicles or documents within the app.

Deleting the app removes local data. If iCloud sync is enabled, data may remain in iCloud until you delete it from within the app or via your iCloud storage settings.
